Who is Dante De Ruwe?
A 20 year old Belgian Student

I study in the city of Ghent, Belgium. A passion for science is what brought me here, to Ghent University, where I studied physics and astronomy for 2 years. Yet; the IT virus had bitten me since my first ever programming class and had taken me over by the end of 2017. I made the decision to change plans and went on to persue a degree in applied information technology at Ghent University College.

A Passionate Developer

I enjoy experimenting, tinkering and just making things work. Often this means teaching myself a whole bunch of new stuff, just as I need them; this has always been my favorite way to learn about new technologies. In my web development I try to make aestheticly pleasing websites, or random fun projects. In Python and Java thinking outside the box to come up with creative software solutions for real world problems is my absolute favorite thing to do.
